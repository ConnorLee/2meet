About Carbon

Carbon is a dynamic price-stable cryptocurrency utilizing machine learning.

Utilizing a toolkit of price-stabilizing methods, instead of just one, Carbon is by-design antifragile and responds to an arbitrary number of market behaviors. Markov-chain optimization and markovian decision processes allows the Carbon protocol to determine which tools to apply, in what quantity, and when.

Welcome to the stablecoin revolution.
